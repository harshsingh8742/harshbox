Stretch Film / Stretch Wrap

Stretch film, also known as stretch wrap, is a flexible plastic packaging material used to secure and protect goods during storage and transit. It tightly binds items on pallets, preventing shifting and damage while offering resistance to dust, moisture, and tampering. Commonly used in logistics, warehousing, and manufacturing, stretch film improves load stability and handling efficiency across supply chains.

Industry usage

Stretch film is used across industries to secure goods on pallets, protect them from dust and moisture, and improve load stability during transport. It's essential in logistics, warehousing, and manufacturing.
