Air Bubble / Bubblewrap Pouches

Air bubble pouches are ready-to-use sleeves that protect fragile items like electronics and glassware. They offer quick packing, shock absorption, and scratch resistance—ideal for e-commerce, retail, and logistics.

Product Features

Excellent Cushioning

Built-in air bubbles provide superior shock and impact protection.

Scratch Resistance

Soft inner surface prevents abrasions on delicate items.

Easy Sealing

Open-top design allows quick sealing with tape or self-adhesive flaps

Lightweight Design

Adds minimal weight to shipments, reducing shipping costs.

Industry usage

Bubble wrap pouches are used in electronics, e-commerce, and retail to quickly protect small, fragile items. Their ready-to-use design offers fast packing, cushioning, and scratch resistance during shipping.
